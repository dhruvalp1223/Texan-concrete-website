  • Talk to Your Houston Concrete Supply About Colored Concrete

    Posted on July 20, 2020 by Phoebee Johnson

    Concrete can come in a variety of colors, like yellow! Talk to your Houston concrete supply about your formula options.

    While concrete is traditionally gray, your Houston concrete supply can also create colored concrete for your project. Colored concrete allows you to create a unique look for your walkway, patio, driveway, or any other concrete structure. Also, many people use colored concrete to make decorative patterns for commercial and residential projects. For the best outcomes, work with an experienced Houston concrete supply for your pigmented concrete.

    What is Colored Concrete?

    Coloring concrete began back in the 1950s and has remained a popular process to this day. Your Houston concrete supply infuses your mixture with pigments to manufacture colored concrete for your projects. These pigments are either mined from the ground or produced in chemical plants. Concrete pigments may come in powder, liquid, or granular form. Then, we combine these products with your Houston ready mix concrete to achieve the look you desire.

    To create your colored ready mix concrete, your Houston concrete supplier mixes cement, water, pigment, and any admixtures needed for your concrete formulation. The type of cement you opt for may affect the color of your concrete. For example, gray cement often produces darker, more muted colors. In contrast, white cement will achieve lighter and brighter concrete. Your concrete contractor will create a custom formula based on your project to achieve the look you are going for.

    How Your Houston Concrete Supply Achieves Consistent Color

    One of the main ways you can ensure that your colored concrete project turns out how you want is to work with an experienced Houston concrete supply company. There are many factors that go into creating color consistency in concrete. Your ready mix contractor will create a concrete formula tailored to your project. Additionally, they can help control any variables that may affect the color, strength, or durability of your concrete. For example, the water ratio of your concrete is essential for color consistency. Therefore, your Houston concrete supply takes steps to keep the water ratio the same for each batch of concrete they create. In some cases, this means incorporating admixtures designed to counteract things like wind and heat that may affect your concrete’s curing process. This industry knowledge and proactive planning from your Houston concrete supply helps create the best results for your colored concrete project.
