    What Is Ready-Mix Concrete?

    Ready mix concrete is formulated by a company that specializes in creating the correct mixture for a wide range of needs. These materials consist of four basic substances:

    • Cements and binders
    • Aggregates like sand and gravel
    • Admixtures, which change the consistency and qualities of the finished concrete
    • Water

    Cement and water are combined to create a paste to which aggregates are added. In some cases, other materials may be added to produce a different texture, color or hardness level. Your Houston ready mix concrete company will ask questions about your project to create the correct formulation for your needs and budget.

    Applications of Ready Mix Concrete

    Ready mix concrete is used to build roads, bridges, high-rise buildings, homes, patios and driveways. This versatile material is well-suited to most building projects and offers durability, energy efficiency and other benefits for projects in the Houston area.
