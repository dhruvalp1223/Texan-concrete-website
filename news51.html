  • Add Safety with Non-Slip Pool Surrounds

    Posted on November 20, 2019 by Phoebee Johnson

    Swimming pools are a great retreat from the Houston summer heat. However, there are risks to having a pool, like slips and falls, so it’s important to create the best possible safety for you, your family, and your guests. For example, your Houston concrete supply company is your best source of information on non-slip surfaces to surround your pool with. There are many options for textured and patterned concrete pool surrounds to help prevent slips and falls. A few basic precautions can help create the safest environment for those who use your pool.

    Secure Your Pool Area With a Locking Fence

    A fence can be the first line of protection for your private pool. Securely locking the fence when you aren’t home or using your pool can add protection against unsupervised swimmers. Houston regulations require all pool fences be at least 4 feet tall. In addition, they must not have any gaps 4 inches or more. If you are installing a new fence, it must be composed of a material other than chain link. Also, the entry point for your swimming pool fence must be self-latching and self-closing. Implementing these safety features will help protect against intrusions and accidents that can result from unauthorized entry to your pool area

    Install a Motion Sensor and Alarm

    Motion sensors are designed to let you know when someone enters your back yard or your pool area. These devices will provide an added line of defense against unwanted intrusions into your pool area. This will allow you to prevent accidental drownings that could occur if unattended children make their way into your pool.

    Opt for a Non-Slip Surround

    Your Houston ready mix contractor has the knowledge and experience needed to make the right recommendations for your swimming pool area. In many cases, your Houston concrete supply company offers formulations specifically designed to provide a safer surface for these areas. Working with an established Houston concrete supplier is the best way to achieve the most attractive and safe poolside solutions. Aggregate finishes, overlays or sealers are all popular ways to upgrade your concrete pool surround and provide you with a safer environment.
