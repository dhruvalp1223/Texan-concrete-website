  • Short Load vs. Houston Ready-Mix Concrete Supply

    Posted on October 25, 2019 by Bizadmin

    Understanding the differences between Houston ready-mix concrete and short-load concrete will help you to make the most appropriate choices for your concrete projects. These concrete delivery options offer specific advantages and drawbacks that may have a significant impact on the quality of your construction process. Working with a qualified concrete supply firm will often provide you with the expert help and guidance you need to choose the right options for your project. Keep in mind a few points when planning a concrete delivery from your local Houston concrete supply company.

    Primary Differences Between Short-Load and Ready-Mix Deliveries

    The most important difference between a short-load and a ready-mix concrete delivery is the location in which the concrete is mixed. For Houston ready-mix concrete deliveries, a full load of concrete is formulated and mixed to strict standards at the plant. Short-load concrete, by contrast, is mixed in smaller quantities by your Houston concrete supplier at your construction location, which limits the supplier’s ability to provide absolute consistency across separate batches of concrete. Each of these types of concrete are designed to suit different needs and to provide different benefits for your project.

    Short Load Deliveries

    Short-load deliveries are mostly reserved for smaller home and business projects that require a very limited amount of concrete and in which durability is not the primary concern. Projects that require less than 12 yards of concrete are best-suited to short-load concrete deliveries. Patios, walkways, small slabs and steps are among the most common short-load concrete projects in our area. For any projects that require a special formulation, reliable durability and consistency of concrete, however, ready-mix concrete options are usually the best choice for construction firms and business owners in our area.

    Ready-Mix Concrete

    If durability, quality and formulation are important considerations for your concrete delivery, your Houston ready-mix concrete company is the best source of this versatile material for your construction needs. Ready-mix concrete is used for a wide range of commercial and industrial construction projects, including concrete buildings, bridges, slabs, retaining walls and footings. This material is ideally suited to larger projects and will eliminate issues with concrete curing before a mix-and-pour process can be completed. This gives ready-mix concrete deliveries the edge over short-load concrete for most larger projects.
