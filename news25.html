    If you have aging concrete, talk to your Houston concrete supplier about your options. In many cases, if you don’t want to completely replace your concrete slabs, an overlay may be the perfect option for you. Concrete overlays involve applying a thin layer of new concrete over an old concrete slab. This can help you repair damaged concrete and improve the look of aging concrete.

    Want a Concrete Overlay? Talk to Your Houston Concrete Supplier

    Concrete overlays are an economical option for restoring, repairing, and enhancing the look of old concrete. Now, you can find overlay products at your local hardware store. However, with your Houston concrete supplier, you can get a customized formula based on your specific needs.

    TDifferent ready mix concrete formulations offer different properties. For instance, you might want a stronger concrete mix for driveways or parking lots that must withstand huge weight loads from vehicles. Yet, if your main goal is to give your concrete floors a facelift, you likely don’t need the concrete to be that strong for your overlay. Your concrete contractor can help you find the best options for your needs.

    In addition, your Houston ready mix contractor can also offer recommendations for the thickness of your concrete overlay. The thickness of the overlay depends on your goals for the project. However, here are some general guidelines based on common projects:

    • Interior floors: 1/16 – 1/8″ thick
    • Driveways: 1/8 – 3/8″ thick

    Benefits of Concrete Overlays

    Concrete overlays, also known as concrete resurfacing, offer many benefits for your aging concrete. This is a great alternative to completely replacing old concrete slabs. Replacing concrete slabs typically involves breaking up and removing the old concrete, finding a place to dispose of the old concrete, performing site prep, creating concrete forms, and pouring the new concrete. This can be expensive and take a lot of effort. Concrete overlays are often faster and more cost-effective.

    Concrete overlays from your Houston concrete supplier can help you repair damaged concrete. For instance, if you notice small cracks or imperfections in your concrete slab, an overlay may improve durability and longevity for old concrete slabs. However, keep in mind that overlays aren’t a good repair option for all damaged slabs. The concrete underneath must be structurally sound, otherwise you’ll have the same issues even after the overlay.

    Also, concrete overlays offer several aesthetic options if you’re looking to restore the look of your old concrete. For instance, you can customize overlays through concrete dye or concrete stamping. You can also polish concrete overlays for a shiny finish.
