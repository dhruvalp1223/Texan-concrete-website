  • Houston Concrete Supply: Concrete Slab for Your Grill

    Posted on December 20, 2021 by Phoebee Johnson

    Our Houston concrete supply can help you create a concrete base for your grill or barbecue.

    Many homeowners love grilling and barbecuing in the backyard. However, it’s important to make sure your grill has the right foundation to sit on. Your Houston concrete supply can help you create the perfect grill pad for safe outdoor cooking. Concrete grill foundations are essential for staying safe while grilling. In this article, we will explain why you need a concrete slab for your grill and how to create one in your yard.

    Why Talk to Your Houston Concrete Supply About Foundations for Your Grill

    You might be wondering why you should have a concrete slab specifically for your grill. This is essential for safe grilling in your yard. Therefore, if you don’t already have a safe foundation for your grill, talk to your Houston concrete supplier about your options.

    Grills need a flat, stable, and hard surface to sit upon. This helps keep them steady to prevent issues for things like the appliance tipping over while you’re cooking. In addition, concrete is naturally fire-resistant, which helps reduce the risks of fires. Most people recommend not putting a grill on grass because not only is it typically not level or stable and also grass is incredibly flammable. Hot grease spills onto grass can easily ignite and cause a fire.

    Now, many people simply place their grills on their patios. However, this can be problematic for many homes because patios are typically really close to the home. Hundreds of fires start each year because of residential grills and barbecues. You want to place your grill at least 10 feet away from your home, shed, and garage (though 25 feet is better). Therefore, in many cases you may need to have a separate concrete slab for your grill, rather than just placing the grill on your patio. This is why you may need to call your Houston concrete supply about pouring a concrete grill foundation.

    How to Create a Concrete Slab for Your Grill or BBQ

    If you need a concrete slab for your grill or barbecue, then your concrete supply can help you formulate Houston ready mix concrete specifically for your needs. You will need to find the right spot to place your grill. Also, you want to make sure you have an extra five inches of space on all four sides compared to your grill for plenty of support.

    Before pouring the concrete, you will need to perform some basic sitework. For example, need to dig into the ground to about the recommended thickness for the slab. You will also need to tamp the ground to ensure it’s compressed. It’s also important to make sure the slab is slightly sloped for drainage. Our team can help you plan for your grill slab before pouring.
