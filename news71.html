  • Pros and Cons of Concrete Floors

    Posted on February 21, 2018 by Bizadmin

    Concrete floors are one of the hottest trends in the flooring marketplace. Working with an experienced Houston concrete contractor can help you achieve the most stunning results when installing these modern floors for your home or office. Here are some of the most important features of concrete flooring for new construction and remodeling projects.

    Versatile

    Concrete floors are available in a wide range of colors and surface textures to suit just about any décor. Concrete can be polished to a glossy shine, making it a visually appealing addition for offices and private homes alike. Dye can be added during the formulation process or applied later as a stain to ensure the greatest versatility for concrete floors. Painted, textured and patterned designs can be applied to create the most stunning and unique look for your Houston home or office.

    Affordable

    Your Houston concrete supply company can provide you with a quote for your flooring project, which will typically be significantly lower than most other premium floor products. This can save you a considerable amount of money on the cost of new flooring and can allow you to enjoy the latest looks for less.

    Professional Installation Required

    Concrete requires expert formulation and installation to create the best results. Choosing an experienced Houston ready mix concrete company can provide you with added confidence in your project and can ensure the most beautiful results for your home or office.

    Cold and Hard

    Some people may dislike the cold and hard surfaces associated with concrete floors, especially in areas used by children and older adults. The hard and unyielding nature of concrete flooring can be mitigated by using throw rugs and carpeting to cushion feet and prevent injuries if a fall should occur.

    Vulnerable to Moisture

    By its very nature, concrete can be vulnerable to moisture. In most cases, your floor will be polished and sealed to reduce the impact of water and other liquids on the surface of your concrete. If excessive water is spilled on the surface, however, or if water intrudes from underneath the floor, your concrete could potentially crumble and deteriorate. Working with an established concrete company can eliminate these issues to ensure optimal durability for your new concrete floor.
